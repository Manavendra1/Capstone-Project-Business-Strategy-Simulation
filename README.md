# Capstone-Project-Business-Strategy-Simulation
The strategic execution and financial performance of Team Navy during a comprehensive global business strategy simulation. Operating across the USA, Europe, and Asia, our company focused on R&amp;D, production, and marketing. Our core mission was to continuously innovate and adapt to rapid technological advancements while expanding our market presence.

Core Objectives:
Ensure financial stability by being profitable.   

Enhance shareholder value to drive growth.   

Expand market share for competitive positioning.   

Minimising the cost of production but ensure the quality of the product.

Strategic Approach
1. Market Prioritization Strategy
Prioritized USA and Asia early, expanding to Europe as markets stabilized.   
Focused on capital efficiency and margin extraction.   

2. Marketing Strategy
Have made the product affordable and market fit.   

Have forecasted the demand using network coverage data & overall market growth rate.   

3. Technology & Product Mix
Launched Tech 1 and Tech 4 with additional features.   

Incorporated a maximum of 9 features at a reasonable rate.   

4. Production Optimization
Balanced in-house and contract manufacturing.   

Focused on minimizing cost and maximizing availability.   

5. Financial Discipline
Maintained strong equity and avoided short-term debt.   
   
Reinvested profits to avoid taxes.   

Have used money for internal transfer within continent as well.   

6. R&D Investment Strategy
Phased R&D with a mix of in-house development and contract manufacturing.   
Learning Outcomes

Risk Management Skills: Gained insight into managing risks across fluctuating costs, taxes and currency values in a global environment.   

Application of MBA: Applied theoretical knowledge from MBA courses like Strategy, Marketing, Finance and Operations in a real-world-like simulation.   

Agility & Adaptability: Learned to pivot strategy based on market changes and competitor actions round by round.
